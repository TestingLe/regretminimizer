## How It Works

Based on Jeff Bezos' **Regret Minimization Framework** - imagine yourself at 80 years old looking back. Which choice will you regret NOT taking?

1. Describe your decision
2. Enter your options (2-3)
3. Get AI analysis of potential regrets
4. Make a choice you'll be at peace with
